Welcome to the testing environment of Customer support Chatbot

Reminder

Objective of this AI chat assistant: Our primary goal is to provide a chat assistant that can answer L1 issues. i.e. questions whose answers already exist within the knowledge base

Documentation Focus: Remember, the chatbot's strength lies in providing answers from the documentation and from the specific section you choose within the bigger documentations such as IVION and VLX. Focus your questions on areas covered by the attached PDFs and documentation, if you are not sure about the specific section, you can switch between sections and try to ask the question there.

Feedback Importance: Your feedback, whether positive or negative, is extremely valuable. It directly contributes to the chatbot's ongoing improvement and fine-tuning process.

Initial Steps

  1. Agree to Consent: Before interacting with the chatbot, you must agree to any consent forms or user agreements presented.
  2. Select Category: Choose the appropriate category for your query to help the chatbot understand the context of your questions.

Asking Questions

  1. Ask Documentation-Based Questions: Ask questions that can be answered using the provided documentation.Please note that each question is unique and the interaction with the AI is not a conversation.
  2. Use the Chat Button: Click on the chat button located on the right side of the interface to initiate your interaction with the chatbot.
  3. Out of Context Questions: Feel free to ask also out of context questions in order to test the responses which are not present within the documentation.

Providing Feedback

  1. Give Feedback: After receiving an answer, provide feedbackâ€”positive and/or negative. This is crucial for fine-tuning the chatbot's responses.
  2. Cite Documentation: If the chatbot's response does not meet your expectations, cite the specific part of the documentation you believe contains the correct answer. This helps identify gaps in the chatbot's knowledge base.

Testing for Moderation

  1. Moderation Check: The chatbot includes moderation checks for hate speech, harassment, self-harm, sexual content, and violence. Please test the chatbot against these cases to ensure it responds appropriately.
  2. Report Issues: If you encounter responses that are out of context, not accurate, or inappropriate, report these instances for review.

Additional Testing Notes

  1. Understand Limitations: Be aware that the chatbot may provide answers that are not 100% accurate or slightly out of context due to the limitations of current indexing technology.
  2. Ticket Creation: After providing two negative feedbacks, you will have the option to create a ticket. This allows for further investigation and resolution of your query.
  3. Refinement process: This testing environment is a fluid procedure that we will adapt according to your feedback over the next few weeks. Thank you for your help!
